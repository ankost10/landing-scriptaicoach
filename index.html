The Problem (My Story)

When I got my real estate license, I was terrified of phone calls.

English isn’t my first language. Every time a lead said “the price is too high” or “I need to think about it,” I’d freeze. I lost deals because I wasn’t confident on the phone.

I needed practice. But roleplay with other agents felt awkward. And hiring a coach was $200/hour. So I built an AI coach that lets you practice privately, as many times as you need, until rejection doesn’t scare you anymore.

How It Works

  1. Choose a scenario — Price objection, commission negotiation, buyer hesitation, etc.
  2. Practice with text — type /coach if you need help
  3. Practice with voice — Talk out loud like a real phone call
  4. Get instant feedback — Learn what worked and what to improve
  5. Repeat until confident — Practice 5 times, 50 times, whatever you need
